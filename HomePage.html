The Fair Housing Council of the Capital Region, Inc.

Who are we?

Why choose us?

We are a non-profit organization that provides education, advocacy, and enforcement of fair housing laws throughout Pennsylvania. Our mission is to ensure equal housing opportunities for all residents, regardless of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability, familial status, or other protected characteristics.

The Fair Housing Council is your advocate working to ensure that you and your family have the right to live in the home of your choice and your dreams. Information is also provided regarding social services and assistance programs throughout the region.
