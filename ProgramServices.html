Landlord/Tenant Counseling Service

Disputes between landlords and tenants are costly for both parties, overburden the court systems and evictions can have a negative impact on neighborhood stability. The Fair Housing Council provides Landlord/Tenant rights information and dispute settlement assistance to landlords and tenants at no cost and without regard to income level. If you need assistance with a rental property issue, please call our office to speak with a landlord/tenant counselor.

If you need eviction prevention services or information , please phone the Fair Housing Council office or send an email to: LHarding@pafairhousing.org

Important: Please include your residence or property Zip Code when submitting an email to the Landlord/Tenant service.

Credit / Debt and Budget Counseling

Do you want to prepare for the financial costs of homeownership? The Fair Housing Council provides one-to-one counseling on pre-purchase topics including:

- Purchase assistance
- Mortgage financing
- Down payment/closing cost fund accumulation
- Accessibility
- Credit improvement
- Debt resolution
- Readiness and preparation for homeownership

If you are interested in our pre-purchase counseling service, please call our office to schedule an appointment.

*This service is provided at no cost, however a small fee for a personal credit report may apply.
